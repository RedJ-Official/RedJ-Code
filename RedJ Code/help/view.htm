View Options

  • Word Wrap: Toggle word wrapping.
  • Word Wrap Auto Indent: Toggle automatic indenttion when word wrapping.
  • Show Folding Lines: Toggle visibility of folding lines.
  • Show Whitespace Characters: Toggle visibility of spaces and newline characters.
  • Highlight Folding Indicator: Toggle highlighting of the folding indicator at caret position.
  • Highlight Current Line: Toggle highlighting of the line at caret position.
  • Highlight Current Word: Toggle highlighting of all occurrances of the current selection or of the word at caret position,
  • Virtual Space: Toggle virtual spaces.
  • Auto Indent: Toggle automatic indentation when typing.
  • Auto Complete Brackets: Toggle automatic completion of bracket pairs when typing.
  • Allow Drag Drop: Toggle dragging and dropping of text inside the editor.
  • Document Map: Show/hide the document map.
  • Ruler: Show/hide the ruler.
  • Autocomplete Menu: Enable/disable the autocomplete menu to show automatically.
  • Tool Strip: Show/hide the tool strip.
  • Status Strip: Show/hide the status strip.
  • Multiline Tabs: Switch between single-line and multi-line tabs.
